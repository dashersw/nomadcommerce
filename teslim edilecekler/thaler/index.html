ABOUT

Thaler is a closed crowd funding network and a growth accelerator for companies, investors and entrepreneurs. At Thaler acquisitions are not only funds but all possible tangible and intangible assets which are valuable for businesses. The core idea within the network is to exchange company equities for assets, manpower, services, strategic contacts and all other definable business accelerators to decrease funding necessities. Using this methodology within a carefully selected company and individual network with well defined roles and responsibilities results a secure path for entrepreneurs to success and a risk-free investment environment for investors. 

TO JOIN

Thaler Network is basically open to all technology companies and business models. Yet there are only two ways to be in the game. For growing companies and service providers it is required to give equities in exchange of assets provided by network companies. For the rest of all raising a fund to create a new business model or extending it at different geographies and market verticals is a must. 

At Thaler acquisitions are not only funds but all possible tangible and intangible assets which are valuable for businesses.

Almost all entrepreneurs and growing companies needs software development services, marketing, sales networks, business developers and landing points around the world. Rather than buying or building those assets Thaler offers an equity exchange to gather necessary services with spending significantly lower budgets or none at all.

For investors Thaler model ensures a risk-free way in which services, technologies and other assets are served with high quality and sustainability avoiding a business model to fail due to peripheral problems such as technology failures, market time, negative marketing or basically not reaching to the right market with right network.

For Entrepreneurs
& Growing Companies

  • Business Model and Necessities are Examined / Business Goals are Defined
  • Budget, Equity Exchange and Responsible Thaler Companies are Decided
  • Reaching Development, Marketing and Sales Milestones Business Model achieves the Goals.
  • Company itself becomes a Thaler Network Member

For Investors
& Fund Owners

  • Looking to all possible and running business models within the network, best model or the company is chosen for the investor.
  • Budget, Equity Exchange and Exit Model are Decided.
  • Reaching Development, Marketing and Sales Milestones invested model or company achieves the Goals.
  • While the company becomes a Thaler Network Member, Investor exists.
